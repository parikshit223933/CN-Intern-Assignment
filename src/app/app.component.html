  • Use "W", "A", "S", "D" keys to play the game.
  • You can also use the swipe gesture to play this game. But remember to swipe only on the game Board, otherwise your swipe action won't be recorded!
  • On each step, either 2 or 4 will be added to a random cell on the game board, you have to get "2048" on the board to win the game.
  • The game Stores Leader Board Data in localstorage.
